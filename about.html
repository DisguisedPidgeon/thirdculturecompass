About Third Culture Compass

Built by a student who moved between four school systems — and couldn't find this resource when they needed it.

The founder

Founded by a Third Culture Kid
"It was rough. But I always kept a positive attitude. I think at some point I became numb to starting over — making new friends, building new routines, figuring out who I was in a place that didn't know me yet. That numbness is real, and nobody warned me about it. That's why this site exists."
Nigeria — grew up in the WAEC system
Malaysia — one year of regular school, then COVID changed everything
San Francisco, CA — 9th & 10th grade at Galileo High School
Amherst, VA — 11th grade and counting

Our principles

01
Lived experience first
Every guide is written from personal experience — not from a textbook, and not by someone who read about this once.
02
Free, always
This information should be free. Students navigating new systems already have enough to deal with.
03
Honest, not inspirational
We don't pretend this is easy. The numbness is real. The resets are hard. We say so.
04
Research-backed over time
We're collecting original data from real students. The findings will be published and public.
